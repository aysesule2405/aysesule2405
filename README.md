> Art taught me to see. Computer science gave me new ways to make.
 
Before I ever wrote a line of code, I was already making things — sketching portraits, pressing clay into shapes, painting light on cheap canvas. When I found CS, something clicked. Not because the two worlds were the same, but because they asked the same first question: *what are you trying to say, and what is the most honest way to say it?*
 
I ship production software, compete in datathons, and keep paint on my hands and clay under my fingernails.
